The Trial Of My Faith

The accident that became a "trial of my faith," not because God wanted me to suffer, but because He wanted me to grow, learn, and to rely on Him in times of struggle. Trials refine us, humble us, and help us turn to God.

Faith is not just believing that God exists but trusting in Him and relying on Him to help us overcome our trials.
