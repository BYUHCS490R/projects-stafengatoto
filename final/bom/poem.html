After the Trial of My Faith

My Story Scriptures Poem

Poem - After The Trials Are Blessings

Never did I ask for pain
nor all the damaged I gain
everything happened in an instant,
that I could hardly even listen
broken glasses lying around
and I wondered if God doesn't love me
then why let this happen now.

My faith was just small then
a tremblling hurting start;
Yet through the hardship I felt
His nearness in my heart.

I later learn a hard lesson,
that this happened for a reason.
Miracles are real
and faith are meant to be tested.
And after every trial, and tears I could not hide
He never left my side.
