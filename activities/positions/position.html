Box Model

  1. What is the difference between block tags and inline tags? - The inline tags stay in the same line as other content and only take up as much space as needed like 'span' or 'a'. But block tags create a big section on a line and takes up full width like 'div' and 'header'.
  2. What is the margin, border, and padding? - Margin is the space outside the border and the border is the line that goes around the text. Padding is the space inside the border.
  3. What are units of measurement you can use in CSS? - Units you can use are px, cm, %, and rem.
  4. When would you use a figure tag? - You can use a figure tag when you want to include a image.
