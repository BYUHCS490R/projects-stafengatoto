Positioning

What is normal flow in HTML? - how elements are automatically placed on the page.

What does float do? Why do you need to clear the float? - Float moves element left or right and lets text wrap around it. It needs to be cleared to stop elements from wrapping around a floated element and restores normal layout flow.

What do absolute, fixed, and relative do? - They are all CSS positioning types that control how an element is placed on a webpage.

How can you make nice navigation buttons for mobile devices? - You use nav tag for your links and you add padding and spacing and coloring to make it look good.
