Why I love Comedy Movies?

Fun to watch and they make us laugh and feel good. In a day full of stress and sadness because the real world has been too hard on you, a comedy movie would be really helpful making you laugh and forget about your sad moments. When we laugh we forget about our problems and feel more relaxed.

Funny situations and characters. You would notice that comedy shows has silly or exaggerated events and characters that are funny to watch.

They help us see things differently. Comedy movies can turn something serious or serious topics into something easier to understand or cope with it.
