Yuki Ishikawa

Interest Yuki Ishikawa

Yuki Ishikawa is the captain of Japan's men's national team. He is my favorite volleyball player of all time. Born in 1995 in Okazaki City. His peak years was 2017 when he led Japan Asian Men's Volleyball Championship gold medal and was named the tournamnet's Most Valueable Player and est Outside Spiker. I always admire how he plays and he can do it all spiking, setting and even passing the ball. He is still playing volleyball and I like watching his matches.
